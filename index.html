The three little pigs

Once upon a time, there were three little pigs. Their names were Jack, Mack and Dack. They lived happily with their mother. But one day, their mother told them it was time to go out into the world. She warned them to watch out for the big bad wolf, and sent them on their way.

Jack, Mack, and Dack walked until they reached a fork in the road. Jack went right. Mack went left. Dack continued on straight. Jack met a man with some straw, which he bought to build a house. It took no time at all. Mack met a man with some sticks, which he bought to build his house. It took a little longer, but not very. Dack bought some bricks and took a long time to build a big, strong house.

THE WOLF ARRIVES

The big bad wolf was getting hungry, approached Jack's straw house, knocked on the door, and shouted:'little pig, little pig, LET ME IN!' Jack replied 'not by the hair on my chinny chin chin' The wolf replied 'then i will huff, and i will puff, and blow your house in! And he huffed, and he puffed, and blew the straw house down. Jack ran away as fast as he could to his brother Mack's stick house.

THE END
